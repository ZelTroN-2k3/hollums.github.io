CONTROLS:
	A,S,D,W - Left, Back, Right, Forward
	Space - Shoot in direction of mouse position
	N + Space or Shift + Space- Activate Nova spell
	T + Click - Activate Timed Bomb at clicked area
	B + Click - Activate Teleport to clicked area
	
The squares at the bottom of the screen will be lit green when the ability is ready to use again.  While on cool-down, the squares will be red and a countdown will initiate until they are ready again.  Player's active spell is colored in blue prior to use.
